Who are we?

The sophisticated player requires a place where their game and their friendships can grow together. Kumla Tennis Club provides an incredible atmosphere to meet your neighbors and enjoy a divine day on the courts. The Club offers options to join competitive teams and intra-club matches. Additionally, extensive tennis clinics and professional instruction are available weekly. With a history of more then 30 years in the business and list of long list customers we dare to say that we might be the best in Sweden.

What do we offer?

More than just a scenic place... Tennis and Pickleball give our members another way to be inspired and grow on the courts. Our six fully lighted courts have been artfully crafted to include amenities most would expect only at a resort. The patio is a great place to meet up with friends before your match. Coffee and refreshments are available each morning, as well as refreshing towels, outdoor heat lamps and sunblock- all within steps of the courts. Built with the sophisticated neighborhood player in mind, the facility inspires members to play at the top of their game.

Lifelong Friendships It's more than just a game. For our members, tennis and pickleball make up an entire community of friends of all ages and skill levels who enjoy friendly court banter and a great match! Getting together in an untraditional setting also brings forth a strong bond of community. Monthly dinner gatherings, round robins, volunteer opportunities and fun club events like our annual Tennis Sneaks and Cleats Tournament allow for members to connect with each other in a completely different way than your standard match.

Player Development Offering tennis and pickleball players more than just a pretty place, Player Development remains a prime focus for our members who have the desire to advance their game. USPTA Coach and Director of Tennis, Chuck Fowler, and his assistant coaching staff maintain stong connections within the local and international tennis community. Most recently, the Club has aquired its very own Pickleball Professional to support the ever-growing game of pickleball. Schedule an hour with Playsight Smart Court for a complete video swing analysis; or download the app for any of our 3 ball machines and track your own stats. Junior clinics and and adult clinics for all skill levels are offered year round, as well as private instruction for both tennis and pickleball.
